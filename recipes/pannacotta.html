Yoghurt panna cotta

Ingredients

  • 3 leaves of gelatine
  • 400 ml single cream
  • 100 g golden caster sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla bean paste
  • 400 g Greek-style yoghurt
  • 400 g strawberries
  • 4 tablespoons elderflower cordial
  • 2 sprigs of mint
  • shortbread or ginger nut biscuits , to serve

Method

  1. GET AHEAD Put the gelatine into a small bowl and cover with cold water. Pour the cream into a pan with the sugar and vanilla paste. Place on a medium-low heat until the mixture just starts to bubble, then turn the heat off. Remove the gelatine leaves from the water and whisk into the pan until melted, followed by the yoghurt. Once smooth and combined, divide between espresso cups, small glasses or moulds, place on a tray lined with kitchen paper (to prevent them sliding about), cool, cover and leave to set in the fridge overnight.
  2. ON THE DAY Hull and halve or quarter the strawberries, depending on their size. Mix with the cordial in a nice bowl and leave to macerate for an hour or so.
